the Third Declension had two forms of case-suffix, (1) -ἑ <from an original

an original ad). Of Consonant-stems with -ῐ in Abl. Sg. we have in
Plautus, eg. parieti Cas. 140 (MSS. -e), pumici Pers. 41 (MSS. -e) (cf. obieci
Pers. 203).
